who am I??

    home about projects

Originally from Los Angeles, California, I made the bold decision to relocate my life to Shanghai where I am currently pursuing a major in Computer Science. Despite initial hesitations from my traditional Vietnamese parents, a curiosity for the world drove me to take a leap of faith.

While I began with plans to major in Creative Business, I found myself drawn to the mysterious and rapidly developing world of tech. I've always been drawn to the potential of AI (mostly from films) and the art of crafting innovative solutions, much like this very website you're reading. With a background in design and the fashion industry, I bring a unique perspective to my work, finding joy in blending creative graphic interfaces with technical competence.

Besides tech, I'm also very interested multimedia art. Alongside a good friend, we founded a school-funded zine focusing on art, film, fashion, and more, printing weekly editions distributed throughout the student body. This initiative allowed me to channel my passion for the arts into a tangible project that allowed me to share my personal tastes and leave an impression on numerous readers.

Furthermore, my interests span a wide spectrum. From a enjoyment for creative writing, evident in the blog that I created, to immersing myself in physical sports like Muay Thai, where I once dedicated two weeks to training in Thailand. Additionally, I also enjoy learning foreign languages, including but not limited to: French, German, Mandarin, and maybe Czech one day, each providing new and diverse perspectives on life.
